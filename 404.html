Authentic Italian Dinner Experience

Immerse yourself in the authentic flavors of Southern Italian cuisine and warm hospitality at The Etna Rosso Ristorante, Fort Lauderdale’s premier Italian dining destination. Our Sicilian-born owner has crafted a menu that showcases fresh, vibrant flavors paired with fine Italian wines, bringing the authentic taste of Sicily to every dish.

When you dine with us, you’ll be enveloped in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere that feels like a journey to Southern Italy. Each bite takes you through traditional Italian dishes, all served in a comfortable and inviting setting, perfect for the best dinner experience.

Warm and Vibrant Dining Atmosphere

Please note: Our outdoor seating is limited, and our indoor dining room is intimate. However, in true Sicilian fashion, the atmosphere can become lively and bustling as diners enjoy their meals and conversations. The close seating and vibrant chatter replicate the authentic experience of dining in Italy, where the convivial spirit is ever-present. Be ready for a vibrant, energetic dining experience that mirrors the warmth of an Italian trattoria—bringing a slice of Italy right to Fort Lauderdale.
