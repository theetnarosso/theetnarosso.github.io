Discover Why The Etna Rosso Is Fort Lauderdale’s Favorite for Authentic Italian Cuisine

With the continued support of our diners, The Etna Rosso Ristorante consistently ranks high in search results for the best Italian restaurant in Fort Lauderdale. This acclaim is a testament to our unwavering commitment to delivering authentic Sicilian cuisine made with the finest ingredients. Our Sicilian owner brings the flavors and soul of his homeland to every dish, ensuring an exceptional dining experience rooted in tradition and passion.

Over the years, we’ve earned rave reviews on Yelp, Google, and TripAdvisor, and are frequently mentioned as a top Italian restaurant in Broward County. Food bloggers and critics alike praise our menu for its authenticity and creativity. As a result, the overwhelmingly positive feedback from our guests has strengthened our reputation, making Etna Rosso a favorite choice for both locals and visitors.

Our warm, trattoria-style setting paired with a diverse selection of house-made pasta, fresh seafood, and curated wines provides the perfect escape to Italy right in the heart of Fort Lauderdale.
