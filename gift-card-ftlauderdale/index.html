Gift Card

Give the gift of a great evening with authentic Italian cuisine, curated wines, and the best dinner experience in Fort Lauderdale.

When you buy a gift card, the recipient receives an email with a QR code that can be used when reserving and dining, allowing them to make a reservation and use the card at their convenience. They can also keep a remaining balance on the card for future visits.

This flexibility ensures they can enjoy their meal whenever it suits them best, making it a delightful and memorable gift. Share the joy of dining at The Etna Rosso and give the gift of a gourmet experience.
