Discover our Diverse Dinner Menu at The Etna Rosso

Our dinner menu features a diverse array of dishes designed to accommodate various dietary needs, including house-made pasta and clearly labeled vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options.

We proudly serve a curated selection of fresh seafood and fish entrées that celebrate the coastal flavors of Italy. Highlights include delicately grilled branzino and traditional Sicilian seafood pasta.

Our menu is thoughtfully crafted so everyone can enjoy a delicious meal in comfort, whether it’s a relaxed date night or a professional business dinner. We prioritize inclusivity and fresh, high-quality ingredients that make every dining experience memorable.

Start your evening with small plates like Sicilian caponata, bruschetta or sautéed mussels and clams cooked in white wine and garlic. If you’re in the mood for something fresh, try one of our salads — perhaps a classic Caprese with locally made mozzarella or the popular Annalisa with walnuts, gorgonzola and mixed greens.

For mains, seafood fans can dive into linguine alle vongole, tagliolini allo scoglio (red or white), lobster ravioli or risotto pescatora.

Our house‑made pasta lineup spans maccheroni Norma with eggplant and Sicilian ricotta salata, basil‑pesto strozzapreti, spicy spaghetti puttanesca, bucatini all’amatriciana, pappardelle al ragù and truffle‑infused bucatini alla gricia.

Meat lovers have options too, including veal piccata in lemon‑butter caper sauce, slow‑braised beef short ribs with roasted vegetables and chicken saltimbocca topped with sage, fontina and capicollo. On the fish side, enjoy pan‑seared branzino, branzino all’acqua pazza poached with tomatoes, garlic and white wine, or the catch of the day.

To complement your meal, our curated wine list highlights Italian reds and whites selected to pair with pasta, seafood and meats. Whether you’re planning a date night, business dinner or family celebration, The Etna Rosso’s warm Sicilian hospitality and high‑quality ingredients create a memorable dining experience that explains our reputation among the best restaurants in Ft Lauderdale.
